The cache is versioned by MAP_VERSION; bumping it (a plot-generation change, or shipping fresh place names) points the server at a new plot-cache/v<n> dir and busts the client ?v=. The named cache is regenerated and pushed by the regenerate-map CI workflow — production can't bake names.
